# Javelin 1.4
## Bug-fix release
This is a minor release fixing a couple of non-critical but very annoying bugs in the game. Thanks a lot to **Greg** who has been helping with excellent bug reports!

**[Download the fixed version here](https://github.com/tukkek/javelin/releases/download/1.5/javelin.zip)**

If any other worthwhile bugs are noticed I will create other bug-fix releases so people can enjoy the game as best as possible. Don’t forget to subscribe to [our news feed](/feed.xml) to stay tuned about further releases!
